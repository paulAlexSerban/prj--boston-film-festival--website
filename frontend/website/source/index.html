Summary

Watch It Outside Events Co. is an organization founded by Clara Dunn committed to the growth of independent artists and to show that films can appeal to audiences of all ages.

Watch It Outside - Boston Edition 2020 is the first edition of an annal film festival organized by Watch It Outside Events Co.

It takes place each August in Boston Public Garden and it includes competitive categories, includes documentary and dramatic films, both feature length and short films, in which awards are given, as well as out-of-competition categories for showcasing new films.
